# LoopForge Product Definition

## Product Name

LoopForge Observe

---

## Category

AI Production Infrastructure

Developer Tool

AI Observability Platform

---

## One Sentence

LoopForge Observe helps developers understand exactly why AI agents fail in production.

---

## Problem

AI agents behave like black boxes.

Developers cannot easily understand:

- Why an agent failed
- Which prompt caused the issue
- Which tool produced an error
- Why retries happened
- How memory affected the decision
- What context the model actually received

Debugging becomes slow, expensive, and unreliable.

---

## Solution

LoopForge Observe records every execution step of an AI workflow.

Developers can inspect, replay, debug, and verify AI executions from one interface.

---
