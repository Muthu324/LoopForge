# Why LoopForge Exists

## Existing AI Frameworks

Today's AI ecosystem makes it easy to build agents.

Examples include:

- LangGraph
- CrewAI
- AutoGen
- OpenAI Agents SDK
- PydanticAI

These frameworks solve orchestration.

They do not solve production operations.

---

## Existing AI Observability

Platforms such as:

- LangSmith
- Langfuse
- Helicone
- Phoenix

provide valuable tracing and evaluation.

However developers continue to report problems with:

- debugging production failures
- replaying executions
- memory visibility
- verification
- operational workflows

---

## LoopForge Position

LoopForge is not another framework.

LoopForge is not another tracing platform.

LoopForge is the production operating layer for AI systems.

Our goal is to make AI systems:

- Observable
- Reliable
- Explainable
- Verifiable
- Deployable

---

## Our Philosophy

Don't replace.

Integrate.

Don't hide complexity.

Explain it.

Don't optimize demos.

Optimize production.
